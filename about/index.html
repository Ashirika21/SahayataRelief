Company Overview

"Sahayta Relief" is a revolutionizing disaster relief management company. It empowers communities to make a meaningful impact during crises. It features a user-friendly interface with powerful tools to streamline efforts. The donation management system ensures a seamless giving experience. Users can securely contribute funds, supplies, and services, with transparent tracking of their impact. Volunteer coordination becomes effortless as individuals register, browse opportunities, and connect with organizations and peers for collaboration and resource sharing.
